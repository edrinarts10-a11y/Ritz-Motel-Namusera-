Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This explains what information we collect through this website and how it's used.

🔒

1. Who We Are

Ritz Motel Namusera ("Ritz Motel", "we", "us") operates this website to provide information about our accommodation, bar & restaurant, and to allow guests to make enquiries, bookings, and leave reviews. We are based at Kampala-Hoima Road, Namusera, Wakiso, Uganda.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with this website:

  • Reviews: Your name, general location (e.g. city), star rating, and review text — submitted voluntarily and displayed publicly on the Reviews page.
  • Bookings & Enquiries: Your name, phone number, preferred room, dates, and number of guests — sent to us via WhatsApp when you use a booking button or form.
  • Chat with Nina: Messages you type into our AI assistant "Nina" are sent to Anthropic (the AI provider) to generate a reply. We do not ask Nina for sensitive personal information, and you should avoid sharing any.
  • Payments (where available): If you pay via Mobile Money or card, your phone number and transaction details are processed by our payment partner (Flutterwave) and the relevant mobile network operator (MTN/Airtel) — we do not store your full payment details ourselves.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to booking enquiries and confirm reservations
  • To display guest reviews publicly to help future guests
  • To provide automated answers via our Nina chat assistant
  • To process payments securely through our payment partners
  • To improve our services and website

4. Third Parties We Work With

  • Supabase — stores submitted reviews
  • Anthropic — powers the Nina chat assistant
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — used for booking and enquiry messages
  • Google Maps — displays our location
  • Flutterwave & Mobile Network Operators — process payments, where applicable

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices. We only share the minimum information needed for them to perform their function.

5. Your Rights

Under Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act (2019), you have the right to ask us what information we hold about you, request corrections, or request that a review you submitted be removed. To do so, contact us using the details below.

6. Data Retention

Reviews remain published until you request removal. Booking enquiries sent via WhatsApp follow WhatsApp's own retention practices. We do not retain payment card or mobile money details — these are handled directly by our payment partners.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
