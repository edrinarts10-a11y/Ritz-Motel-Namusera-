✦ Who We Are

A Fresh Stay
Worth Every Night

Ritz Motel is a modern hospitality destination along Kampala-Hoima Road in Namusera, just after Shell Petro Station. Whether travelling for business or leisure, we offer a clean, comfortable and affordable experience.

From well-furnished rooms to our garden lounge — perfect for birthdays, get-togethers, and weddings — plus a vibrant bar and restaurant, every corner of Ritz is designed for your relaxation.
